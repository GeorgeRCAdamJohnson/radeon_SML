Risk Mitigation

Technical Risks

  • LLM Costs: Smart caching and model optimization to control API expenses
  • Data Quality: Automated validation and human review processes
  • Scalability: Cloud-native architecture with auto-scaling capabilities
  • Security: Zero-trust architecture and regular security audits

Business Risks

  • Competition: Focus on unique domain expertise and superior user experience
  • Market Changes: Flexible architecture enabling rapid pivoting
  • Regulatory: Proactive compliance and ethical AI practices
  • Funding: Phased development approach with clear milestone gates

Recommendation
